About IEDC

Innovation & Entrepreneurship Development Cell

The IEDC (Innovation & Entrepreneurship Development Cell) at College of Engineering Vadakara is a government-backed initiative by Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) to foster an entrepreneurial culture among engineering students.

We host workshops, hackathons, mentoring sessions, and startup boot camps throughout the academic year, helping students at every stage of their entrepreneurial journey.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the most common questions about the bootcamp.

Who can participate?
Any engineering student — whether from CEV or other colleges across Kerala — is welcome to register and participate. No prior startup experience needed!
Is registration free?
Yes! The IEDC Startup Bootcamp is completely free to attend. Just register through the Google Form and secure your spot.
Do I need a team to register?
No. You can register as an individual and teams will be formed on the day of the event. You're also welcome to come with a pre-formed team of 2–4 members.
What should I bring?
Your laptop, your ideas, and your enthusiasm! We'll handle the rest — venue, internet, stationery, and refreshments.
Will certificates be given?
Yes! All participants will receive participation certificates. Winners and runners-up will receive special recognition certificates from IEDC.
Do I need a startup idea before coming?
Not necessarily. The bootcamp includes an ideation session where you'll brainstorm with your team. If you already have an idea, even better — bring it!
