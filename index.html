Who We Are

What is the Startup Bootcamp?

The IEDC Startup Bootcamp is a one-day intensive program designed to ignite the entrepreneurial spark in engineering students across Kerala.

🎯

Mission-Driven

We empower student innovators to validate ideas, prototype solutions, and present to real-world mentors.

🌐

Open to All

IEDC members and students from any college across Kerala are welcome to participate and connect.

🏆

Compete & Win

Pitch your startup idea to a panel of judges and win exciting prizes, mentorship, and incubation support.

🤝

Network & Grow

Build meaningful connections with mentors, industry leaders, and fellow innovators over one power-packed day.
