Venue Details

College of Engineering Vadakara

Vadakara, Kozhikode District, Kerala – 673104. The bootcamp will be held in the main seminar hall and connected lab spaces.

How to Get There

CEV is well-connected by KSRTC buses from Kozhikode and Kannur. Nearest railway station: Vadakara (5 km). Auto and taxi available from station.

🅿️

Parking

Free parking is available within the campus for two-wheelers and four-wheelers. Follow the IEDC signage from the main gate.

🥗

Food & Refreshments

Lunch and tea breaks are included. The college canteen will also be open throughout the day for additional snacks.
